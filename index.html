Fyllin Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 18, 2025

Overview

Fyllin is a Chrome extension designed to help users fill web forms using AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the extension works.

Data Collection

Fyllin does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. We do not have servers that receive your information.

What Fyllin Processes Locally

The following data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to us:

  • Form Field Information: When you use the autofill feature, Fyllin reads form field labels and attributes from the current webpage to understand what data is needed.
  • Uploaded Documents: When you upload documents (PDF, images, text files) for extraction, all processing (including OCR) happens locally in your browser.

What Fyllin Sends to Third-Party AI Providers

When you use the AI form filling feature, Fyllin sends the following to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic, or Groq):

  • Form field labels and types (e.g., "First Name", "Email", "Phone")
  • Document text (if using the "Fill from Document" feature)

Important: You must provide your own API key from these providers. Fyllin communicates directly with the AI provider's API using your key. We never see or have access to your API key or the data exchanged.

Data Storage

Fyllin stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage:

  • API Key: Your AI provider API key (stored securely in chrome.storage.sync)
  • Settings: Your selected provider, model, and custom instructions

This data:

  • Is stored only on your device(s)
  • Syncs across your Chrome browsers if you're signed into Chrome
  • Is never accessible to us or any third party

Third-Party Services

Fyllin integrates with the following third-party AI services (based on your choice):

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Groq Privacy Policy

Permissions

Fyllin requests the following Chrome permissions:

Permission Purpose
activeTab Access the current page to read form fields and fill values
storage Store your settings and API key locally

No Tracking

Fyllin does not include:

  • Analytics or tracking scripts
  • Advertising
  • User behavior monitoring
  • External resource loading

Children's Privacy

Fyllin is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
